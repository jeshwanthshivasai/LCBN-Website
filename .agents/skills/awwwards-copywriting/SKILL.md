## Core Philosophy
Show, don't tell. Premium websites use minimal, punchy copy that gets straight to the value proposition.

1. **The Headline**: Short, active, and outcome-oriented. 
   - *Bad*: Our software helps you manage circular economy initiatives easily and effectively.
   - *Good*: Master the Circular Economy.
2. **Subtext**: 1-2 sentences explaining *how*. No fluff.
3. **Microcopy**: Buttons should be active verbs. ("Start Building", "Explore Platform", not "Click Here").
4. **Technical Authority**: For B2B, use industry-standard terms confidently, but avoid dense paragraphs of jargon. Break complex ideas into scannable bullet points or animated graphics.
